# 8 color scheme options

all mockups for intent solutions io landing page

## dark themes (premium feel)

**01-dark-professional.html** - purple on black (like intelligent ai studios)
- background: #0a0a0a
- accent: #8b5cf6 purple
- vibe: enterprise, sophisticated

**04-midnight-blue.html** - cyan/blue on navy
- background: #0f172a slate
- accent: #0ea5e9 sky blue
- vibe: tech-forward, modern

**05-forest-green.html** - green on dark forest
- background: #0c1810 dark green
- accent: #22c55e emerald
- vibe: growth, sustainable

**06-sunset-orange.html** - orange on charcoal
- background: #1c0a00 dark brown
- accent: #ea580c orange
- vibe: energetic, bold

**07-charcoal-slate.html** - gray monochrome
- background: #18181b zinc
- accent: #e4e4e7 light gray
- vibe: minimal, professional

## light themes (approachable feel)

**02-polished-casual.html** - purple on cream
- background: #fafaf9 warm white
- accent: #8b5cf6 purple
- vibe: friendly, approachable

**03-high-contrast-minimal.html** - pure black/white
- background: #000000 pure black
- accent: #3b82f6 blue
- vibe: bold, design-forward

**08-warm-cream.html** - amber on cream
- background: #fffbeb warm cream
- accent: #d97706 amber
- vibe: warm, inviting

---

## recommendations by use case

**for enterprise clients**: 01 (dark professional) or 07 (charcoal slate)
**for sports parents**: 02 (polished casual) or 08 (warm cream)
**for both audiences**: 01 (dark professional) - premium but not cold
**most unique**: 05 (forest green) or 06 (sunset orange)
**safest choice**: 01 (dark professional)
